    Our Team

    This page briefly goes into the background of our team members.

    I am Louis Rauch, a junior at the University of Notre Dame from Chicago, IL. I am a Finance major with minors in Data Science and Italian. On campus, I am involved in Student International Business Council (SIBC) and the Jubilee Initiative for Financial Inclusion (JIFFI).

    Growing up in New Orleans, Logan attended public schools until matriculating to Notre Dame. During high school, the board of education elected Logan to their Student Leadership Council, where he worked closely with then-Superintendent Dr. Cade Brumley (now leads Louisiana Department of Education) to share the opinions of marginalized student voices and offer a student perspective on district policy decisions. The first in his family to attend college, and a Business Analytics major, Logan is passionate about leveraging education and data analytics to improve the lives of students from historically underrepresented communities.
